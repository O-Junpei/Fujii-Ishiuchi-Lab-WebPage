We are conducting research on molecular spectroscopy using ultrashort laser pulses having a time width of nanoseconds, picoseconds to femtoseconds and its application. In addition to investigating the structure and reaction mechanism of molecular clusters, solutions and molecules adsorbed on solid surfaces by making use of time-resolved spectroscopy and nonlinear spectroscopy, we are developing a new spectroscopic method for application.
