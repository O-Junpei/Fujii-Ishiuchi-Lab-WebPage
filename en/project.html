Science of soft molecular system functions by cooperation between theory and experiment

http://www.yawaraka.org/


Although substances form a hierarchical structure from a single molecule to a cell, the current frontier of chemistry can be said to be in the elucidation and creation of the functions of complex systems. The essence of a complex system with a high function typified by a biomolecule system is that it has a large internal degree of freedom and that the system flexibly changes according to the situation and develops the optimum function. We define a complex molecular system with such characteristics as "soft molecular system" and conduct research that integrates molecular science, biophysics, synthetic chemistry, theory and computational science towards understanding and control of its function I will. Specifically, we conduct a research that combines the soft molecular system typified by biomolecules, supramolecules, molecular aggregates, interfaces, etc. and its element processes with the three approaches of theoretical calculation, advanced measurement, and function creation , We will create a new academic field of "molecular science" for complex systems.

Railway Construction and Transport Facilities Development Support Organization (JRTT) Contract Research

"Study on hazardous organic matter real time evaluation method in automobile exhaust gas by portable laser ionization analyzer"


There are chemical species harmful to the human body even in trace amounts in hydrocarbon (HC) discharged from automobiles, and its emission varies depending on the driving situation, but the current regulations remain limited to the total amount of HC. Considering health effects, exhaust gas assessment according to the toxicity of each component, which changes according to the driving situation, is required. However, in the high sensitivity measurement of the exhaust gas component by the existing method, pretreatment such as separation and concentration is necessary, and concentration change of trace components due to operating conditions can not be measured in real time. Therefore, we focused on the laser ionization method which enables molecular selection, high sensitivity, real-time measurement, real-time highly sensitive measurement of trace amounts of hydrocarbons at the laboratory level, but even the measurement technology in the real environment has been reached not here.

Therefore, in this research, we developed a portable laser ionization analyzer with a practical equipment configuration that can be widely used in general environments, grasp the actual condition of trace harmful components contained in automotive exhaust gas using it, and establish its evaluation method By collaboration of industry, government and academia.

Tokyo Tech Integrated Research Institute (Super COE)

Environment Project

"Reduction of the risk of combustion exhaust gas to health - Establishment of real-time measurement and evaluation technology of trace chemical substances" -

http://www.iri.titech.ac.jp/


This project aims to realize a safe and secure living environment by measuring in real time the trace amounts of chemical substances present in the environment. This real-time pretreatment-free high sensitivity analytical technique is realized by supersonic jet laser multi-photon ionization method (Jet-REMPI method) accumulated by laser molecular spectroscopy research. In the Jet-REMPI method, since the laser wavelength is synchronized with the resonance line of the target atom / molecule and only the objective component can be selectively ionized and mass analyzed, it has high detection sensitivity and substance identification ability even when contaminants are present It is a feature.

New Energy and Industrial Technology Development Organization (NEDO) Industrial Technology Research Grant Project

"Development of High Pressure High Speed ??Switching Pulse Valve for Supercritical Fluid Jet and Mass Spectrometry and Application to Laser Spectroscopy of Nonvolatile / Thermally Decomposable Sample"


The supercritical fluid jet method is a method of dissolving a nonvolatile / thermally decomposable sample in a supercritical fluid and directly jetting it in a vacuum to vaporize these samples nondestructively without heating it to high temperature. This research aims to develop a high pressure fast opening and closing pulse valve for supercritical fluid jet generation and a mass spectrometer using the high pressure fast opening pulse pulse valve and also analyzes biological macromolecules and nanotechnology molecules which are difficult to measure by the conventional technique also by spectroscopic analysis We are aiming for prototyping as a possible new mass spectrometer. In addition, we apply this equipment to gas phase laser spectroscopy measurement of various bio-related molecules.
